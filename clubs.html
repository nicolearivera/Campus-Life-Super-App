Requirements

How to Join a Club

01
Enrollment Verification

You must be currently enrolled at State University with at least 6 credit hours per semester to join any officially recognized club or organization.

02
GPA Requirement

Most clubs require a minimum GPA of 2.0. Competitive and Greek-life organizations may require 2.5 or higher. Check individual club pages for specifics.

03
Dues & Fees

Some clubs collect membership dues, typically $10–$50/semester. Financial assistance is available through the Student Activities office for eligible students.

04
Attend a Meeting

Most clubs welcome new members at the start of each semester. Check the Events calendar for club fairs and open meetings.

🚀 Want to start a new club?

Any group of 5+ students can apply to register a new organization. You'll need a faculty advisor, a constitution, and approval from the Student Activities Board.
